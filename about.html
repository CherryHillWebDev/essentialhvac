About Us

Family-Owned Heating & Air Company Serving Burlington, Camden & Gloucester Counties

At Essential Heating and Air, we provide reliable heating repair, professional heating installation, and fast emergency HVAC service throughout South Jersey, including Burlington County, Camden County, and Gloucester County.

We offer fair and honest pricing for replacement and repair of most brands of heating and air conditioning equipment. All work is guaranteed and full sataisfaction is assured to all our customers.

We practice honest business practices paired with quality service. There are no hidden fees or “catches”. We offer value pricing to all our current and new customers. We believe in old fashion values; when a hand shake was as good as a contract and courtesy and honesty was the norm.

Honest, Respectful Service You Can Trust

Our philosophy is simple. Treat the customer the way you would like to be treated. We respect our customers and realize they work hard for their money, just like we do. With that in mind, we perform our service diligently, thoroughly and with professionalism while respecting you and your property.

We never "up sell" or try to sell you what you don't need or want. We provide what we would expect as a customer and strive to deliver more than promised. We will make suggestions when appropriate and let the customer decide what suits their needs and budget best. We want our customers to feel certain they received great service and have been treated with respect. We feel it's common sense, treat the customer like you would like to be treated.
